# Marketing Dashboard

## Objective
Track marketing campaign performance across channels to optimize spend and ROI.

## Key Metrics
- Campaign impressions and reach
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Conversion rate
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs)

## Tools & Techniques
- Power BI
- DAX measures for campaign KPIs
- SQL for data extraction and transformation
- Data modeling and relationships
- Conditional formatting for KPI alerts

## Business Impact
- Provided real-time insights into campaign effectiveness
- Helped marketing team optimize budgets across channels
- Increased campaign ROI by identifying high-performing segments
